About Islam & Muslim Culture

Islam is a religion of peace that teaches belief in one God (Allah) and following the guidance brought by the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ.

The Five Pillars of Islam

Muslim life is built on five main pillars:

1. Shahadah – Belief and testimony that there is no God but Allah and Muhammad is His Messenger.

2. Salah – Performing the five daily prayers.

3. Zakat – Giving charity to help those in need.

4. Sawm – Fasting during the month of Ramadan.

5. Hajj – Pilgrimage to Makkah for those who are able.

Muslim Culture

Muslim culture is rich with values such as respect for parents, kindness to neighbors, modesty, generosity, and love for learning.

Around the world, Muslims may speak different languages and wear different clothing, but they are united by the same faith and core values.

Purpose of This Website

This page is created for educational purposes to give students a basic, respectful introduction to Islam and Muslim life.
